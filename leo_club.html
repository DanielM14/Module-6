What is Leo CLub

Leo Club is a club that volunteers to help the comminuty and it people.
This is also a good way to get to know new people and make friends.

I was approached about getting a LEO club here at West about 7 to 8 years ago,
because I have always run a volunteer club. So the Davenport Breakfast Lions wanted to see who could help them run it.
There were 2 other teachers who are now not here that helped get it started.
The idea is that we just want to help people, so we help wherever we can! - Ms.Hansen (Leo Club Leader)
